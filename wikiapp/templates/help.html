Using the Site

How do I sign up?

  1. Click 'join' at the top right of the page.
  2. Add your email address and password.
  3. Click 'join'.
  4. You will be redirected to the homepage.
  5. Enjoy!

How do I login?

  1. Click the 'login' button at the top right of the page.
  2. Fill in your email address and password.
  3. Click 'sign in'.
  4. Enjoy!

How do I edit a page?

  1. You may only edit a page if you are already logged into your account.
  2. Click the edit button that is next to the page header.
  3. Click save when you are done.
  4. You can change and edit the text to make headers and lists etc. You can either do this through markup or you can use the editing bar at the top of the textbox

How do I use markup?

  1. Here are some useful shortcuts for markdown
  2. # H1
  3. ## H2
  4. ### H3
  5. #### H4
  6. ##### H5
  7. ###### H6
  8. ** text ** to bold text
  9. * to create an unordered list
  10. You can find more information on markdown Here

How do I create a new page?

  1. You may only create a page if you are logged in (either as a Guest or into your own account).
  2. Click on the 'All' tab at the top left of the page
  3. Choose the country that you want to add a page for from the dropdown list
  4. Click 'add page'
