About us

Wiki Journey is a non-profit organisatation dedicated to bringing you relevant and up-to-date information through our extensive user network. Wiki Journey users collaborate to provide information on the topic that we love: travel. Wiki Journey was started in 2015 by a group of postgraduate students in sunny South Africa.We currently have more than 10 000 active users with profiles on countries on all 7 continents.

We love our users

We rely on input from users like you, whether you are a jet setter or an armchair surfer. We greatly appreciate all the time and effort you put into helping fellow travellers to enjoy their travel experiences.
